### Dashboard
- Staff detail and edit option
- Next shift date and time
- How much tip they have

### All Shifts Page
- All shifts for that specific staff with attendance statuses

### Kitchen Display Page
- A list of orders with their necessary data
- An option to update order status

### Ready Page
- Interactive large full screen page with an option to show ready orders. The ready orders will each be visible for 3 minutes and if a new one gets added they will be two and not more than two.

### Payment Receiving Cashier Page
- Customers can pay at the cashier
- Order food there too.
